This section: managing a difficult situation

Other options you can consider

Health conditions and disabilities can be different from person to person, so treat each employee's needs individually. An employee with an undiagnosed condition will have very different needs from one managing chronic pain, for example.

Legal obligations

To meet your legal obligations you need to have considered or put into practice as many of the following as possible:

  • discussed what your employee can do and what they find difficult
  • had regular discussions together about what is and is not working
  • put reasonable adjustments in place - read more information about this on the Acas website (opens in a new tab)
  • contacted organisations such as Acas, HSE or Equality Advisory and Support Services (EASS) for independent advice or support
  • let your employee know about possible financial help available to them

If the situation has become difficult to manage

You could consider legal guidance or advice if a situation has become difficult.
