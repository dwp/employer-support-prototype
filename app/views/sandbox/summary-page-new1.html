Paying your employee

Key information
You must pay Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) if your employee is eligible.

Having conversations with your employee

Key information
As an employer, you have a duty to protect your employee’s health, safety and welfare. This comes under the Health and Safety Law.

You must make sure that you are following the GDPR.
Useful links
(opens in new tabs)
  • Check more information about employers' health and safety law responsibilities on the HSE website
  • Check what is classed as direct and indirect discrimination on the Equality and Human Rights Commission website
  • Find out more about the GDPR on GOV.UK
  • Workplace policy templates on the Acas website

Considering adjustments and additional help

Key information
If your employee has a disability, you must make reasonable adjustments if they would otherwise be substantially disadvantaged in doing their job.
