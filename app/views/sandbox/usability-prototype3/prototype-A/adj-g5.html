Get expert workplace health support

There can be times when a situation is complex, or you need extra support. Occupational health professionals can help you manage health and wellbeing at work.

How they can help

Occupational health experts can help with employee health by:

  • providing health assessments for your employees
  • helping you decide on any changes you might need to make at work
  • supporting your employees to manage their health

They also prepare your business for any future issues by:

  • managing and monitoring cases over time
  • helping you to create policies for your business

Benefits for your business

There is a cost to using these services but they can have many benefits for your business. 

Helping your employees to stay healthy can:

  • reduce absences
  • prevent problems getting worse
  • save money in the long run by taking the right actions as early as you can

There is more about using occupational health services on the Acas website (opens in new tab).

Finding occupational health services

The following organisations can direct you to services that could help you.

  • Society of Occupational Medicine (opens in new tab) has a tool to help you find a local provider.
  • Commercial Occupational Health Providers’ Association (opens in new tab) has a contact form for local providers
  • Safe Effective Quality Occupational Health Service (opens in new tab) has a list of services that meet their quality standards
  • NHS Health and Work Network (opens in new tab) has a map of local NHS providers
