Your duty as an employer

As an employer, you have a duty to protect your employees.

You must make sure:

  • you keep a record of any discussions you have with your employee
  • you have considered or put into practice reasonable adjustments

What is ‘reasonable’ in terms of adjustments will depend on your business. You can find examples of adjustment on these pages (all links open in a new tab):

  • Discuss changes to ways of working
  • Discussing changes to work environment
  • Discussing extra help
