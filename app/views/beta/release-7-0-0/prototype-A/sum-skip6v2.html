The guidance on this page highlights what you have seen throughout the service. It includes your legal obligations as an employer, examples of reasonable adjustments and links to further guidance from other organisations.

Your legal responsibilities

! Warning As an employer, you have a duty to protect your employees.  

You must make sure:

  • you keep a record of any discussions you have with your employee
  • let your employee know about possible financial help available to them
  • you have considered or put into practice reasonable adjustments

What is ‘reasonable’ in terms of adjustments will depend on your business. You can find examples of adjustments on these pages (all links open in a new tab):

  • Discussing changes to ways of working
  • Discussing changes to work environment
  • Discussing extra help

Possible financial assistance
