Your legal responsibilities

! Warning As an employer, you have a duty to protect your employees.  

You must make sure:

  • you keep a record of any discussions you have with your employee
  • let your employee know about possible financial help available to them
  • you have considered or put into practice reasonable adjustments

What is ‘reasonable’ in terms of adjustments will depend on your business. Check examples of changes at work you could put into practice:

  • ways of working (opens in new tab)
  • work environment (opens in new tab)
  • extra support and temporary and permanent changes (opens in new tab)

Possible financial assistance
