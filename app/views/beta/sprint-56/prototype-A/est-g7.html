This section: Paying your employee

You do not have to pay your employee Statutory Sick Pay

Your employee has been off work for more than 28 weeks. This includes non-working days.

! Warning Your employee is no longer eligible for Statutory Sick Pay (SSP). You do not have to pay SSP.

SSP stops being paid to employees after a period of 28 weeks. Some employers choose to continue to pay their own sick pay after this time.

Possible financial help for your employee

You can let your employee know that they might be able to get financial help from the government. They will need to apply for this themselves.

Check more about SSP payments on GOV.UK (opens in new tab).
