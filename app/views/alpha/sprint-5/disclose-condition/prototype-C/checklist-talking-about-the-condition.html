Checklist: talking about the condition

It’s important to talk to your employee as soon as you can. These topics can help you have an open and useful conversation.

Ask about them first

Start by asking about your employee in general - find out how they’re feeling.

Example: “Thanks for letting me know...” “How are you in general?”

Will they stay at work?

Discuss if staying at work would help, or if they might need sick leave.

Example: “Would it help to take some time off?” “If you’d rather stay, we can work something out”

Job and workplace

Ask how the condition will affect their job, work environment and ways of working.

Example: “Has your job made things worse?” “What do you think you’ll be able to do for the next few weeks?”

Changes to help

Talk about any changes to their job or workplace that might help. They could be short or long-term.

Example: “What kind of changes can we make together?” “Tell me what would help you, we’re listening...”

Check in with your employee as often as works for you both. You can update this sheet or start a new one.
