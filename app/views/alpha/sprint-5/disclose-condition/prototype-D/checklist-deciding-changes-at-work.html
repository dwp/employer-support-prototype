Checklist: deciding on changes at work

Changes to the workplace or the job can help people stay at work while they manage their health. This checklist can help you to decide on the changes they might need.

Changes to the environment

This kind of change might include things like desk space, equipment and other physical changes.

Example: an employee who uses a wheelchair might need an accessible car parking space.

Ways of working

Changes to work hours, shift patterns or other policies at work can help.

Example: if a condition affects an employeeâ€™s energy in the morning, they might need to start their working day later.

Extra help

It might help you employee to have someone to help with parts of their job or how they access work.

Example: someone with an eyesight condition might need someone they work with to read their post for them.

Check in with your employee as often as works for you both. You can update this sheet or start a new one.
