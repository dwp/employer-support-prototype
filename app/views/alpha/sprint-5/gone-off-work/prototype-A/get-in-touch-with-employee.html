You should get in touch with your employee

Your employee has been off work for 8 days.

You should send them an informal message or call them. The purpose of your contact should be to see how they are, not to ask when they are coming back.

Staying in regular contact with your employee is the best way to help them come back to work when they are ready.

If your employee comes back to work too soon, they might relapse and need more time off work.
